Brownies

This brownie recipe is super simple, super consistent, and is ready to eat within 35 minutes

Equipment

  • 8x8 inch baking pan
  • parchment paper
  • cake tester

Ingredients

  • 145g unsalted butter
  • 250g granulated sugar
  • 80g cocoa powder
  • 1/4 tsp kosher salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 65g all-purpose flour

Method

  1. Heat the oven to 165℃ and line the baking pan with parchment paper, buttering the exposed sides
  2. Melt together the butter, sugar, and cocoa powder in the microwave
  3. Once lukewarm whisk in the eggs, vanilla, and salt
  4. Fold in the flour, being careful not to overwork the batter at this point
  5. Pour into pan and bake for 20-25 minutes or until the cake tester comes out clean
  6. Let rest for 10 minutes before removing from the pan and serving
